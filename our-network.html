Our Network

In 2000, His Late Highness Prince Karim Aga Khan IV initiated the establishment of the Aga Khan Academies, an integrated, global network of schools dedicated to expanding access to the highest standard of education. The first Academy opened in Mombasa, Kenya in 2003, followed by Hyderabad, India in 2011, Maputo, Mozambique in 2013 and Dhaka, Bangladesh in 2022.
